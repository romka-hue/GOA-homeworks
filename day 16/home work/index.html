Tournament Overview

Bow and arrow tournaments are exciting events where skilled archers from around the world compete. Some of the most popular tournaments include the World Archery Championships and the Olympics.

Tournament Categories

There are various types of archery tournaments, including:

  • Target Archery: Archers shoot at stationary targets.
  • Field Archery: Archers navigate a course in natural settings and shoot at different targets.
  • 3D Archery: Uses 3D animal targets, simulating hunting scenarios.
